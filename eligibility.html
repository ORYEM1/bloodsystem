Blood Donation Eligibility

General Requirements

  • Age: Must be between 17 and 65 years old.
  • Weight: At least 50 kg (110 lbs).
  • Health: Must be in good general health.

Specific Requirements

  • Recent surgery or major illness: May disqualify you from donating.
  • Medications: Certain medications may prevent donation.
  • Pregnancy: Not eligible to donate during pregnancy.
  • Travel: Recent travel to certain countries may affect eligibility.

Other Considerations

  • Tattoo/Piercing: Must wait 6 months after getting a tattoo or piercing.
  • Cold/Flu: Must be symptom-free for at least 72 hours.
  • Alcohol: Avoid alcohol 24 hours before donation.
